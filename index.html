What I do

I take the commercial problems that drive revenue, cost, and growth decisions — the kind consultants spend weeks diagnosing — and turn them into structured, interactive decision systems anyone can operate. Each tool compresses deep domain expertise into something you can run in minutes.

The evidence

Seven diagnostic tools across card payments and platform economics. Each takes a problem that usually lives inside consulting decks — and structures it into explicit inputs, testable rules, and actionable outputs. Payments is the proving ground.
